## Purpose

POCO Toolkit is designed to help users safely analyze and maintain stock POCO devices through Android Debug Bridge (ADB).

The project follows a safety-first workflow:

**Scan → Analyze → Debloat → Restore → Verify**

The toolkit prioritizes reversible changes and informed decisions over aggressive debloating.

## Device Compatibility

Every Android device is different.

Recommendations generated by POCO Toolkit are based on device analysis whenever possible.

Users should avoid applying generated scripts to devices that were not analyzed first.

---

## Privacy

POCO Toolkit is designed with privacy in mind.

Device scan reports are intended for local analysis and user review.

The project does not intentionally collect, transmit, or monetize personal information.

---

## Project Philosophy

The goal of POCO Toolkit is not to remove as many applications as possible.

Its purpose is to help users understand their devices and perform safe, reversible maintenance while preserving stability, compatibility, and everyday usability.
